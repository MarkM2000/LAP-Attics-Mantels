Definitions

  • Attic: a room or area directly beneath a building's roof.
  • Garrett: an attic or top floor room, particularly a tiny, dreary one (usually where an artist lives).
  • Loft: a raised space or upper story in a building, frequently featuring high ceilings and open areas.
